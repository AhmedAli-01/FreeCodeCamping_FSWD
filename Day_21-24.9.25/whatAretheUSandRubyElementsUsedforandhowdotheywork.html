What Are the U, S, and Ruby Elements Used For, and How Do They Work?

The unarticulated annotation element, or u element for short, is used to represent inline text that has non-textual annotaiton applied. Here is an example of using the u element to highlight various spelling errors: 

<p>
  You can use the unarticulated annotation element to highlight
  <u>inccccort</u> <u>spling</u> <u>issses</u>.
</p>
